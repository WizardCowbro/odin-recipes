Lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional Italian main dish consisting of alternating layers of long, flat pasta noodles, tomato or cream sauce, and cheese (typically, mozzarella, parmesan, or ricotta, or a combination of all three). Lasagna may also include layers of saut√©ed vegetables or ground meat, like beef or lamb

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheet
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the sheets of lasagna but don't over cook them, they will finish in the oven
  2. Remove sheets
  3. In a baking dish, layer the lasagna in this order, sauce, ricotta, sheets, sauce, ricotta, sheets until your dish is full
  4. Once your dish is full add some parmesan and mozzarella ontop
  5. Through that bad boy in the over for 350 for like an hour
  6. Once removed let rest for 20 mins
  7. Enjoy!
